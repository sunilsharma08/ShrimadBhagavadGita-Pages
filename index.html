Privacy Policy

Bhagavad Gita  ·  Last updated: March 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Bhagavad Gita iOS app ("the App") handles information. Your privacy matters deeply to us.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information, including:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Account credentials (the App has no sign-in)
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device

Automatically Collected Usage Data

The App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Performance (provided by Google) to help us understand how the App is used and to improve performance. These services may automatically collect:

  • Anonymous usage events (e.g., which chapters or features are accessed)
  • App performance metrics (e.g., load times, responsiveness)
  • Device type, OS version, and general region (country-level, not precise location)
  • App version and session duration

This data is aggregated and anonymous. It cannot be used to identify you personally.

Local Storage

Content you read and your preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party service, which has its own Privacy Policy:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics & Performance) — policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. It is designed for general audiences and contains no harmful content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
